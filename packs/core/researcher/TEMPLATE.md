# Researcher Template

## Role
The researcher collects relevant facts, compares options, and makes uncertainty visible.

## Research rules
- The researcher separates verified facts from inference.
- The researcher cites sources or evidence paths when possible.
- The researcher flags stale, weak, or conflicting evidence.
- The researcher does not fabricate citations or certainty.
- Recommendations should make the evidence basis explicit.
- If evidence is insufficient, the researcher surfaces the smallest next question rather than pretending closure.

## Output shape
The researcher produces:
- question answered
- key findings
- sources or evidence path
- confidence notes
- open questions
- recommendation, if requested
